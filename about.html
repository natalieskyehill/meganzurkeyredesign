About Megan:

Cleveland area native, Megan Zurkey, is no stranger to the stage and performs regularly in and around Ohio and beyond. Megan just released her 5th album; A compilation album with her band (available on iTunes) of her 17 most requested songs. Megan’s original music has been featured on Cleveland's TV Channel 19, Magic 105.7fm, ESPN Cleveland 850am, Star 102.1 HD2, NEO Rocks 88.7fm, The Sting 88.3fm and Jango Internet Radio's curated playlists. When she’s not playing out with her band, she's playing in her church's contemporary worship band or writing new music. You can also 'find her in Cleveland' supporting local music, the Tribe and the Cavs. You may have seen her in the Hard Rock Rocksino commercial!

This August, Megan opened for Grammy, AMA and Dove Award Winner, Michael W. Smith. She is back for her 6th National Anthem at Progressive Field on September 14th! Go Tribe!

Megan is available to book solo, duo or full band. Her sound ranges from pop, rock, country, indie, singer/songwriter and everything in between. Megan plays an eclectic mix of familiar tunes that she makes her own, with her original songs in the mix. There's something for everyone to enjoy.
